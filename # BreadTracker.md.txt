# BreadTracker

Full-stack grocery optimization application built with:

- FastAPI (Python backend)
- PostgreSQL + SQLAlchemy Async
- Google GenAI (Gemini integration)
- Next.js 16 (Frontend)
- Node.js 20 LTS
